Design Principles

Heather Schmidtlein

PARC: Repetition

MoMA: moma.org


Repetition is found within the repeating pattern of block sequences in MoMA's mobile design. Each block alternates the image, text, and background color to the left or the right. The bold font is repeated throughout the page to showcase important information to the user.

White Space

Duolingo: duolingo.com


Duolingo utilizes white space surrounding its immediate call to action for the user. This helps the eyes to focus in on what they would like you to do rather than get distracted by elements surrounding the text.

Visual Hierarchy

Squarespace: squarespace.com


Visual hierarchy is present in Squarespace's mobile design. Visually, the reader is guided down through the text from the top. The heading stands out first, afterwhich the call-to-action button, followed by details regarding signing up as well as an example image.
