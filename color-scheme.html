COLOR SCHEME

Our color scheme references the colors of a clear sky with moody blues, a soft cloud white, and a popping sunny yellow.

#4F6D7A (Cadet) is used for the body text and logo text because of its good visibility.

#C0D6DF (Columbia Blue) is used for the background color as well as the hover color for links.

#F5F5F5 (Cultured) is used as the section background for the body text along with the header and footer background.

#EAA821 (Marigold) is used for items that need to stand out, such as headers and links.
